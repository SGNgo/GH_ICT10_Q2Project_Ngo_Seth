ABOUT OUR SCHOOL

Hawkins High School stands as the pride of Indiana. This institution is full of school spirit, mysteries, and legendary students. Known for its strong academics, energetic students, and unforgettable history, the school continues to shape the next generation of adventurers.


Whether you're joining the basketball team, the AV Club, or just trying to survive another day in the Upside Down, Hawkins High creates an environment where every student can grow, learn, and thrive.
